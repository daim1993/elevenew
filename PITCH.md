# Elevé — one-page pitch

**What:** a beautiful studio site *and* a browser-native CAD room planner with a materials library — a lead magnet that converts visitors into design clients, plus a standalone tool with its own subscription.

**Who:** boutique interior/architecture studios, and prosumers planning a space.

**Why it wins:**
- Instant, install-free CAD in the browser — draw, furnish (real mm blocks on proper CAD layers), see it in 3D, get a costed spec, export DXF/OBJ/PDF.
- A memorable brand experience (interactive grid, handpan UI, page-turning materials book) that few competitors match.
- Content-managed marketing site so the studio updates it themselves.

**Model:** Studio Free (5 cloud projects) → Studio Pro (unlimited, share links, priority) at ~$24/mo; agency/team seats later.

**Moat (to build):** CAD interoperability (DWG/IFC round-trip), a curated material + supplier catalogue, and template libraries.

**Status:** working product foundation with a real backend, accounts, cloud projects, versioning, sharing, and exports. Next: payments, integrations, real content, and go-to-market. See ROADMAP.md.

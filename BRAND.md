# Elevé — brand guidelines (working)

**Positioning:** elegant, minimalist, material-led design studio. Quiet confidence, technical-blueprint precision.

## Colour (monochrome)
- Ink `#17181b` · Ink-soft `#3c3e44` · Muted `#8b8f97`
- Backgrounds `#edeff2` / `#f4f5f7` / `#e8eaee` · Card `#f6f7f9` · Line `#d7d9de`
- No brand colour accents; no AI purple/pink gradients. Contrast > 4.5:1 for body text.

## Type
- Display / headings: **Playfair Display** (500–600).
- Body / UI: **Inter** (300–500).
- Mono / technical: **JetBrains Mono** (labels, dimensions, tags).
- Headings use `text-wrap: balance`; generous letter-spacing on eyebrows/labels.

## Form
- **Squared corners everywhere** (border-radius: 0). Thin 1px hairlines. Poché-black walls in plans.
- Motion: soft, 150–300ms, `cubic-bezier(.22,1,.36,1)`; always respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Interactive grid background + custom square cursor are signature — keep subtle.

## Voice
- Understated, precise, sensory. "Quiet", "material-led", "considered". Avoid hype and emoji in UI.
